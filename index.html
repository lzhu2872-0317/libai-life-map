Li Bai Life Map

Tracing Li Bai's Travels and Changing Poetic Imagery through TEI, Voyant Tools, and Map Visualisation

This capstone project explores how Li Bai's travel experiences are reflected in his poetry. The project focuses on place names, travel routes, and changing poetic imagery in selected poems. Instead of treating Li Bai's poems only as literary texts, this project uses digital humanities tools to show how geography and imagery are connected in his writing.

The project will use TEI encoding to mark important textual elements, such as poem titles, place names, natural images, emotional expressions, and travel-related words. Voyant Tools will be used to identify frequent terms and patterns in the poems. A simple map visualisation will also be created to show selected places connected to Li Bai's travels. Through these tools, the project aims to present Li Bai's poetry in a more visual and exploratory way.

Core Research Questions

  • What place names and travel-related words appear in the selected poems of Li Bai?
  • How do the poetic images change across different places or travel contexts?
  • How to use TEI encoding to help the project organize place names, imagery, and textual features in Li Bai's poems and ultimately digitize them?
  • How can Voyant Tools and map visualisation support a clearer understanding of Li Bai's poetic travels?
